To-Do List

How to Use?

This JavaScript To Do list is fairly easy to use


Adding To Do's

First step is to type what you want to have in to do list. After that, click on the button ADD.


Changing to do's

To change preferred to do, all you have to do is choose which one. The first to do you type have the index 0, second to do have index 1, and so on. After you choose desired to do, change it to whatever you want, and click CHANGE TODO button.


Toggle Completed

Principle stays the same as CHANGE TODO. Pick the desired to do, insert index, and click TOGGLE COMPLETED.
